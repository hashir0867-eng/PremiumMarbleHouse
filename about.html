Our Philosophy

We treat each slab as a one-off artwork. Our process combines curated sourcing, high-precision CNC fabrication and hand finishing to produce installations that read beautifully in daylight and under curated lighting. We advise architects and designers on vein direction, book-matching and finish selection so that installations feel cohesive across multiple rooms.

What sets us apart

  • Direct quarry relationships — better slab selection and pricing transparency
  • Precision fabrication — tight tolerances, mitered joins and CNC waterjet inlay
  • Insured, trackable shipping — shock sensors for high-value crates
  • Aftercare & maintenance guidance — lifetime advice for major installs

Fabrication & CNC workflows ▾

We run a nested cutting algorithm to maximize slab yield and plan book-matching sequences as part of the nesting. Edge profiling is done on dedicated machines and all seams are test-assembled in the shop when possible. For mitered waterfall edges, we use jigs to maintain exact 45° tolerances and inspect under high-intensity lighting before packing.

Testing & Certification ▾

We partner with accredited labs for water absorption, density and mechanical strength testing. For projects with façade exposure, we require lab certificates and mock-ups before approving the final slab batch.

Logistics & Crating ▾

Crates are built with treated timber, internal padding and shock monitoring when requested. High-value shipments include photos at dispatch and a recorded loading sequence for insurance claims. Site lift plans and A-frame locations are agreed with site teams prior to arrival.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we view slabs at your yard?

Yes — we schedule slab viewings by appointment and recommend bringing finish samples so we can recommend slabs that match color & vein direction.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we manage export documentation, crating and shipping. Customs duties and import taxes vary by destination.

How long is lead time?

Typical domestic lead time: 2–6 weeks depending on fabrication complexity. International deliveries depend on sea/air freight schedules.

Do you offer warranties?

We provide limited warranties for fabrication defects. Natural variation in stone is not considered a defect — we document slabs and require client sign-off for selected faces.

Certifications & Sustainability

We publish a yearly sustainability summary: offcut usage, yield improvements and selected quarry practices. We also facilitate third-party environmental audits on request.
