Our Services — Procurement to Installation

Comprehensive services for architecture, interior fit-out, and bespoke residential projects — sourcing, fabrication, technical support, and long-term aftercare. We coordinate slab selection, precision fabrication, certified installation and post-install maintenance with transparent reporting and robust logistics.

Procurement & Slab Selection

Direct quarry partnerships and slab-level photography allow you to approve exact faces. We manage customs paperwork, certificates, and logistics coordination for international slabs.

  • Slab inspection & grade reports
  • Book-matching selection and dry-lays
  • Sample boxes & yard viewings by appointment

Precision Fabrication

State-of-the-art CNC, waterjet inlay, and controlled polishing lines. We provide mitered joins, integrated sinks, and reinforcement for thin slabs where required.

  • CNC routing & 1-2mm tolerances
  • Waterjet for inlays & drainage channels
  • Hand finishing for leathered/honed surfaces

Installation & On-site Work

Certified installation teams with engineered fixing systems for cantilevered islands, cladding, and large-format slabs. Includes on-site supervision & installation plans.

  • Laser verification & templating
  • Anchors, adhesives & grout specs
  • Complex book-match supervision

Aftercare & Maintenance

Professional sealing, maintenance schedules, and lifetime care advice. We offer on-site re-polishing & restoration services for damages and etches.

Design Consultation

Material-led advisory covering vein direction, finish selection, backlighting strategies for onyx, and collaboration with lighting designers for optimal visual effect.

3D Visualization & Mock-ups

Photoreal renders and physical mock-ups to help designers and clients visualise final installations, including vein-matching previews and finish proofs.

  • Photoreal renders and AR previews
  • Physical full-size mock-ups for critical junctions
  • Lighting & finish simulation

Export Logistics & Customs

We manage export documentation, crate certification, and international freight partners to ensure slabs arrive without damage and on schedule.

  • Crate design & shock monitoring
  • Door-to-door freight coordination
  • Insurance & claims support

Specialist Restoration & Conservation

Conservation-grade restoration for heritage stone and modern architectural pieces — from micro-fill repairs to full-surface re-polishing.

  • Etch & scratch remediation
  • Structural repair & anchoring
  • Protective treatments for chemical resistance

Custom Stone Design

Bespoke fabrication, book-matched statement surfaces, and integrated stone furniture — design-led execution for high-end interiors.

  • Custom furniture & sculptural work
  • Integrated lighting & resin inlays
  • Large-format spanning and reinforcement

Our Process — How we work

1
Discovery & Brief
We review drawings, budgets and finish intent; create a project scope and schedule.
2
Quarry & Slab Selection
We propose slabs with photos, lot numbers and book-match options. Client selects exact faces.
3
Fabrication & Mock-up
CNC programming, edge profiles, test-fitting and mockups for critical pieces.
4
Shipping & Crating
Certified crates, photos at dispatch, shock monitoring and scheduled deliveries.
5
Installation & Handover
Site supervision, precision survey and final client sign-off with care instructions.
